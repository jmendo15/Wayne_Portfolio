Biography

I'm Wayne

I’m a NY/LA based director, photographer, and writer. Above all, I am a storyteller.

I was lucky enough to find my place in the collective, WinWithoutWeapons LLC, and have created alongside my closest friends for 3 years, honing my craft and expanding my knowledge on the art of visual storytelling. I feel ready to take on the world and do my part to create during a time when the world needs inspiration! Let me help you tell your story and bring your visions to life!
